Better Dogs is a comprehensive social and behavioral overhaul of the Minecraft Wolf for Minecraft 26.1+ (Java 25). It replaces basic wolf AI with a high-performance, event-driven scheduler featuring personality intelligence (Aggressive, Pacifist, Normal), social bonding, genetics-based breeding (litter systems), smart survival safety (avoiding cliffs, creepers, and lava), autonomous feeding, and dynamic wild pack territoriality with leader duels. Fully compatible with C2ME and performance-optimized via DasikLibrary 1.7.0.
