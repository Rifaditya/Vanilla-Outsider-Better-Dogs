### 2. The Lava Test

* **Setup:** Build a safe platform near lava. Command a wolf to move near the edge. Capture it stopping or looking away.
* **Alt Text:** "A wolf standing safely at the edge of a lava pool, refusing to jump in."
* **Caption:** "Smart AI prevents tragic lava accidents."

### 3. Creeper Danger

* **Setup:** Spawn a creeper near a wolf. Capture the moment the wolf turns to run away.
* **Alt Text:** "A wolf running away from a creeper that is about to explode."
* **Caption:** "Wolves know when to flee from danger."
