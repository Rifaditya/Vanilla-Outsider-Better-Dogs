# A Developer's Reflection: 6 Months of Better Dogs

**Date:** June 29, 2026

I just had a sudden realization today—I have been working on **Better Dogs** for over 6 months! 

It's crazy to think about how much time has passed and how far this project has come. What started as a simple idea to make Minecraft wolves a little bit smarter has evolved into an incredibly complex, personality-driven social AI system. 

From wrestling with the math behind the dynamic pack spread scaling, to designing the Nemesis system and figuring out the intricacies of the Jade UI, it has been an absolute journey. The fact that the mod now supports custom advancements, literal genetics, litters, and completely custom patrol patterns without breaking the vanilla aesthetic is something to be really proud of.

Time really flies when you're deeply invested in a passion project. Here's to all the late-night debugging sessions, the relentless pursuit of "Vanilla Outsider" perfection, and to making wolves truly worthy companions!
